About US Donuts:

US Donuts is conveniently located on N Highland. North Highland connects the northside of Jackson with the 45 Bypass and helps visitors from Humbolt and Medina pass through. Because of the location of US Donuts, visitors passing through on their early morning to work can stop on the way and grab a quick breakfast. US Donuts opened in this area because there were no breakfast shops around, and believe everyone should start off their day with a delicious breakfast. Hope to see you soon!
