About US Donuts:


US Donuts opened its doors in the winter of 2013. Ever since day one, we have been making fresh donuts daily and we still make them by hand every early morning. The donuts' recipe has changed slightly over the years to have the perfect balance of sweetness and an airy dough. We have been told the donuts' comforting taste reminds them of home, which is perfect for someone who has to have their early morning breakfast away from home. The early mornings at the shop are great before work or school because we believe everyone should start off their day with a delicious breakfast. We do offer other foods other than donuts, like croissants, kolaches, and biscuits. The beverages ranges from dairy to juices and to coffee. US Donuts are looking forward to satisfy all your cravings.
